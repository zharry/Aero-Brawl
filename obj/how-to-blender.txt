Red: X
Green: Y
Blue: Z

Modifiers
x - lock x
y lock y
z - lock z
shift x - lock y, z
shift y - lock x, z
shift z - lock x, y

Commands
left click - move cursor
right click - select obj
middle click - rotate
shift middle click - pan
shift f - minecraft mode (esc to leave)

shift - select multipule
ctrl g - group

s - scale
g - move
tab - switch mode
shift d - duplicate
